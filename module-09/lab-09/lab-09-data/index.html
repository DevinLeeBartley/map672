Zero Emission Power Plants in the United States

An Interactive map

Map authored by Devin Bartley

This map displays solar, wind, and nuclear power plants in the united states. The size of the circle for each plant is porportional to the total generation capacity. Clicking in an area will filter the map to a 500 km radius. If you click on an individual power plant, more information about the plant will display. If you click on your 500 km radius, a filter will appear showing the total generation capacity in the filter area. Data source: US Energy Information Administration
