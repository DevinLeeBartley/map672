Two Important Kentucky Cities

And the Tiny City Between Them

Map authored by Devin Bartley.

The town of Midway is of equal distance between Frankfort, the Capital of Kentucky and the city of Lexington. It is also located at an equal distance between Varsailles and Georgetown. Source: wikipedia.
