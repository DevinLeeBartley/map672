A Digital Representation of the Surface of the Earth

This simplistic representational image of the earth demonstrates that the convention of the cartographic gaze has become highly engrained in our culture's phsyche. What is shown here instantly triggers the perception of an understanding in our minds about how the world's continents, oceans, and landmasses are laid out. What we should remember is that this often taken for granted representation of the globe hasn't been the standard for all human cultures and is a relatively new way of visualizing the planet we live on.
